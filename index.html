Our Comprehensive Approach

We follow a complete farm-to-market cycle that ensures farmers receive maximum support and fair pricing at every stage.

🌱 Phase 1: Seed Selection

We provide certified, high-quality seeds selected specifically for Maharashtra's climate. Our focus on premium varieties like Khapli Wheat ensures better yields and market demand.

📚 Phase 2: Guidance & Training

Expert agricultural advisors guide farmers through every stage - soil preparation, sowing techniques, pest management, and harvesting. Regular field visits and training sessions ensure best practices.

🏪 Phase 3: Direct Procurement

Once crops are ready, we directly purchase produce at guaranteed competitive prices. No stress about market fluctuations - your price is assured!

💰 Phase 4: Fair Pricing

Cut out middlemen and earn maximum profit. Our direct purchasing model ensures farmers get 85-90% of retail market prices instead of 40-50% through traditional channels.

Our Products

🌾 Khapli (Khafali) Wheat

Khapli Wheat is an ancient Indian heritage crop known for its exceptional nutritional value, higher protein content, and better taste compared to modern wheat varieties.

  • Higher protein content (13-15% VS 10-12% in regular wheat)
  • Rich in minerals and amino acids
  • Better for health-conscious consumers
  • Premium market demand
  • Excellent shelf life

Our Complete Services

Comprehensive agricultural solutions from seed to sale

Current Offering: Khapli Wheat, Premium Maize Hybrids

👨‍🌾 Expert Farming Guidance

Dedicated agricultural experts provide personalized guidance. From soil testing to pest management, disease identification to harvesting techniques - we support every step of the farming cycle.

Services: Field visits, Training camps, WhatsApp support, Crisis management

We encourage modern farming techniques while respecting traditional knowledge. IoT soil sensors, drip irrigation advice, weather forecasting, and precision agriculture methods.
